SMASH Your LIMITS

Step onto the court, embrace the challenge, and discover the thrill of badminton with BMU Shuttlers.

JOIN US

Whether you're a seasoned badminton player or a complete beginner, we encourage you to join our vibrant community of enthusiasts. We welcome students of all skill levels and backgrounds, eager to share our passion for the sport and create a supportive and encouraging environment.

Our Services

BMU Shuttlers is committed to providing our members with a comprehensive range of services to support their badminton endeavours. We offer:
Coaching and Training

• Structured practices and drills led by experienced coaches
• Specialized coaching for players of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced
• Personalized instruction to address individual strengths and weaknesses
• Tactical and strategic guidance to enhance performance
• Emphasis on proper technique, footwork, and conditioning 

Facilities and Equipment

• Access to top-notch badminton courts with high-quality flooring and lighting
• Availability of badminton nets, rackets, and other necessary equipment
• Well-maintained facilities that ensure a safe and enjoyable playing experience
• Dedicated areas for warm-up, stretching, and post-match recovery 
• Access to physiotherapy services for injury prevention and rehabilitation

Tournaments and Competitions

• Opportunities to participate in intercollegiate tournaments and competitions
• Representation of the college in badminton events at regional and national levels
• Organization of internal tournaments and leagues to foster friendly competition and camaraderie
• Support and encouragement for members seeking to pursue competitive badminton 

About the BMU Shuttlers

Our Mission

BMU Shuttlers is dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for students of all skill levels to experience the joys of badminton. We strive to foster a community of passionate badminton enthusiasts who are committed to personal growth, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Our Values

Inclusivity: We embrace diversity and encourage participation from all students, regardless of their background or experience.
Passion: We ignite a passion for badminton among students, inspiring them to pursue their athletic goals and appreciate the sport's unique qualities.
Excellence: We promote a culture of excellence, encouraging our members to constantly strive for improvement and personal growth.
Sportsmanship: We uphold the highest standards of sportsmanship, fostering a respectful and competitive environment that promotes camaraderie and fair play. 

Our Commitment

BMU Shuttlers is committed to providing students with the resources and support they need to succeed in their badminton endeavours.We offer:

• Weekly Practices and Drills: Our experienced coaches lead structured practices designed to improve your skills, technique, and fitness.
• Intercollegiate Tournaments: Compete against badminton clubs from other colleges and universities, showcasing your skills and gaining valuable competitive experience.
• Social Events and Outings: Beyond the court, we organise social gatherings and outings to strengthen bonds, foster friendships, and create lasting memories.
• Volunteering Opportunities: Give back to the community and share your passion for badminton by volunteering at local events and programs.
